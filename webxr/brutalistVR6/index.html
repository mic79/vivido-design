brutalistVR6

Fork of brutalistVR5 with multi-bounce path-traced lightmap bake: every texel runs a real Monte Carlo path tracer (NEE sun + cosine-weighted bounces, default 3) instead of v5's direct-only + constant fill. Light propagates through openings into enclosed volumes. Knob: ?bvhMaxBounces=N (3 = open scenes, 5–6 = enclosed rooms). Same UI, same Sky+bloom, separate IndexedDB store from v5.
VR Stereo Preview: press B on the right controller in VR to render a stereoscopic 360° panorama at your current position (path-traced, progressive). Press B again to exit and store the viewpoint as a glowing sphere. Walk back within 1 m and press B to re-enter. Quality: ?vrpreviewres=low|medium|high|max (default medium). Samples per eye: ?vrsamples=N. Max stored viewpoints: ?maxPanoramas=N (default 3).
