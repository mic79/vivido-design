VR RUNNER

Room → hallway → obstacle. Earth gravity, one air jump. Throw direction matches where you face (same as brutalistVR8). Grab-throw only on the ground or at a wall (wall jump). End of hall: full glass wall, then a 6 m air gap and a 10×10 m neighbor roof — miss it and fall far enough → run resets (glass, arrows, combat). Fridge: 45°. B toggles combat (or HUD).

Map: omit ?map= or use ?map=1 for the default streaming sandbox (100 m 3D cells, 27 wireframe+cube cells, centre spawn). ?map=0 — furnished room / hallway. The pizzaplex ~105k-tri GLB is on by default: sector shells appear at once; GLB work is queued and spread across frames so streaming stays smooth. Your current cell uses the full GLB; other cells use merged+Lambert. ?pizzaplex=0 disables. ?sectormerge=0 (or ?roofmerge=0) skips merge on non-player cells.
