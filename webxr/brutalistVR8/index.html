brutalistVR8 — sector streaming

Procedural 9×9 grid of 80 m sectors (81 unique compositions). The 3×3 around the player streams in/out as you move; everything else is unloaded. Real-time sun + shadows only — no lightmap baking. Locomotion: Nock-style grab/throw + moon gravity, Y on left controller toggles editor flight.
Combat: click Combat: OFF below or press X on the left controller. Right trigger fires; hold left trigger (or G) to aim a grenade, release to throw.
URL params: ?sun=4 sun intensity, ?diff=easy|normal|hard|insane, ?mode=endless, ?seed=daily, ?hdr=1 for sky background.
